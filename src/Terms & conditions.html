About SLEVS

SLEVS is a secure government portal run by the Department of Immigration & Emigration (DI&E) of the Democratic Socialist Republic of
Sri Lanka.

Processing time

The processing time for visas quoted by SLEVS system is normally 3 (three) working days. Processing time should be calculated starting from
the next business day after your application's online submission day through SLEVS system. (DI&E) is entitled to request additional information
and documents, which may cause delay of the application concerned.

Fees and Payments

I hereby agree to pay all fees and charges for consular services required by SLEVS system. By “E-payments” - an electronic system for State
duties and payments, it is possible to pay the fees for consular services. In order to make a payment with the “E-payments” system - VISA,
MasterCard, American Express, Discover card or PayPal Account is required.

Please, note that the Visa fee is non-refundable.

I acknowledge that the granting of visa is a sovereign right of the issuing country. Its refusal is not subject to any refund or appeal except
in cases provided by the law.

The main reasons for refusal of the visa are stipulated by the Law of the Sri Lanka on Foreigners.
